Discover Climate Change

What is Climate Change?

🌡️

Global Warming

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns. While climate changes are natural, human activities have been the main driver since the 1800s.

🏭

Human Impact

Burning fossil fuels like coal, oil, and gas produces greenhouse gases that trap heat in our atmosphere, causing global temperatures to rise rapidly.

🌊

Environmental Effects

Rising sea levels, extreme weather events, droughts, and floods are all consequences of climate change affecting communities worldwide, especially in Ghana.

🇬🇭

Ghana's Challenge

Ghana faces rising temperatures, changing rainfall patterns, and coastal erosion. These changes threaten our agriculture, water resources, and coastal communities.

👥

Youth Action

Young people are the key to solving climate change. Your energy, creativity, and passion can drive the changes needed to protect our planet's future.

💚

Solutions Exist

Renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, reforestation, and lifestyle changes can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and build climate resilience.
